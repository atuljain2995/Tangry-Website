## 2. Social Media Marketing

### Platform-Specific Strategy

**Posting Schedule**: 4-5 posts/week + 1-2 Reels/day

**Content Pillars**:
1. **Recipe Videos** (30-40%):
   - 30-60 second Reels
   - Quick recipes using your spices
   - Behind-the-scenes cooking
   - User-generated content

2. **Product Showcases** (20-30%):
   - Flat lay photography
   - Product benefits
   - New launches
   - Packaging shots

3. **Educational Content** (20-30%):
   - Spice facts
   - Health benefits
   - Storage tips
   - Cooking techniques

4. **Engagement** (10-20%):
   - Polls and questions
   - User recipes
   - Contests
   - Behind-the-scenes

**Hashtag Strategy**:

## 7. Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics to Track

#### Website Analytics (Google Analytics 4)
- **Traffic Sources**: Organic, social, direct, referral
- **Top Pages**: Which products get most views
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitors to customers
- **Average Order Value**: Track over time
- **Bounce Rate**: Optimize high-bounce pages

#### SEO Metrics (Google Search Console)
- **Impressions**: How many times site appears in search
- **Clicks**: People clicking through
- **CTR**: Click-through rate
- **Average Position**: Rankings
- **Top Queries**: What people search for

#### Social Media Metrics
**Instagram**:
- Followers growth
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / followers)
- Reach and impressions
- Profile visits ‚Üí Website clicks
- Best performing content types

**YouTube**:
- Views
- Watch time
- Subscribers
- Click-through rate to website

#### Email Marketing Metrics
- **Open Rate**: Target 20-25%
- **Click Rate**: Target 2-3%
- **Conversion Rate**: Emails to purchases
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Keep below 0.5%

#### Ecommerce Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Target 1-3%
- **Cart Abandonment**: Target < 70%
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Total marketing / new customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Average repeat purchases
- **Return Rate**: Product quality indicator
